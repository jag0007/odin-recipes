Meatball Bolognese

A meaty red sauce to pair with your favorite pasta!

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Yellow Onion
  • 4 Cellery Stalks
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 8 oz. Cherry Tomatoes
  • 8 oz. Mushrooms
  • 6 cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1/2 lb. Ground Pork
  • 24 oz. Canned Spaghetti Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoke Parpika

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dice the onions, cellery and bell pepper and set aside together
  2. Roughly chop the cherry tomoatoes and mushrooms and mince the garlic
  3. Combine the ground beef and ground pork
  4. Season the meat with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder and smoked papika
  5. Make 1 inch diameter meatballs from the seasoned meat
  6. Add some olive oil to a pan, along with the diced onion, cellery and pepper
  7. Bring pan to a medium heat
  8. Add the tomoatoes and mushrooms to the pan once the peppers and onions have caramelized and the celery is soft
  9. Let cook for five minutes, then add halve of the garlic, cook for one minute, then remove all vegtables from the pan
  10. Put olive in pan along with the remaining garlic, and cook until fragrent
  11. Add meatballs to the pan, rotating them to ensure they cook thoroughly without burning
  12. Once the meatballs are finished, return the vegatables to the pan with the meatballs, and pour in the spaghetti sauce
  13. Let simmer for at least fifteen minutes
  14. Enjoy atop your favorite pasta!
