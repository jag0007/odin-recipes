Spaghetti

Description

Quick, easy and tasty, this classic recipe for spaghetti will leave you full and satisfied!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Directions

  1. Heat butter in a pan
  2. Season the ground beef with salt, peeper, garlic powder and onion powder
  3. Brown the ground beef in the pan
  4. Once the ground beef is cooked, add the spaghetti sauce to the pan with the ground beef
  5. Reduce heat for the pan to low, and let simmer while cooking the noodles
  6. Heat water in pot to a rolling boil
  7. Place noodles in the boiling water and set a timer for 5 minutes
  8. When the timer goes off, drain the water from the noodles
  9. Serve up the noodles into a bowl, and add meat and sauce
  10. Enjoy!
