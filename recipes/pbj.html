PB&J Sandwich

Description

The classic pb&j. Perfect for lunches togo, or anytime you need a quick snack It's simple, and endlessly custumizable. Want whole wheat bread? You got it! Prefer strawberry jam to grape jelly? No problem! It's your sandwich, your way

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly/Jam
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Get out two slices of bread
  2. Using a knife, spread some peanut butter (or whatever your nut butter of choice) on one slice of bread
  3. Using a knife, spread the your choice of jelly/jam on the other slice of bread
  4. Place one slice of bread onto the other, peanut butter and jelly sides together
  5. Using a knife, cut the sandwich straight down the middle, from top to bottom
  6. Enjoy!
